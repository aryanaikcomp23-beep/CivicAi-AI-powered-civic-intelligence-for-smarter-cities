How CivicAI Works

9-step AI pipeline — every action saved to local database in real-time

STEP 01
📸

Issue Reporting

Upload photo with auto GPS. Complaint saved to local DB with a unique ID and timestamp.

CITIZEN FACING
STEP 02
🔬

Feature Extraction

AI extracts object type & severity. Feature vectors stored in Atlas for downstream clustering.

AI MODULE
STEP 03
🔗

AI Clustering & Dedup

Queries local DB for nearby reports. Duplicate tickets merged into existing geo-clusters.

KEY MODULE
STEP 04
⚡

Priority Scoring

Score calculated from current DB data: severity, cluster size, location sensitivity, recurrence.

AI MODULE
STEP 05
👷

Task Assignment

Assignment written to local DB. Team receives photo, GPS, SLA — all sourced from database records.

AUTOMATED
STEP 06
🔮

Hotspot Prediction

Aggregation pipelines on historical data generate risk-zone forecasts and heatmaps.

PREDICTIVE
STEP 07
🚨

Auto Escalation

SLA breach writes escalation record to DB. Full 3-level log for transparency.

ACCOUNTABILITY
STEP 08
✅

Resolution & Verification

Before/after saved to DB. Citizen rating stored. AI confirms resolution via image diff.

AI VERIFIED
STEP 09
📊

Dashboard & Analytics

Live queries from local DB — real issues, real escalations, real hotspots, real KPIs.
